Explorer

Provides you with everything that you need to excel during your varsity years!

How it all began?

When I was a fresher, I had a lot of questions in mind. I wanted to learn, but I felt a lack of guidance. University life is very different from school life. Everything picks up a pace and often, one might feel lost amidst this chaos.

Explorer is a one-of-its-kind initiative to bridge this gap of mentorship and resources. We want our juniors to feel confident in what they do. For all the Chitkarians, by the Chitkarians!

Be curious.

- Kushal Bhanot
